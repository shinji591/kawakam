Climbing tour course list

Recommended climbing time

We recommend the following times for the Kawakami Village mountain climbing tour. These times are perfect to enjoy the beautiful mountain views and comfortable temperatures.

9am to 12pm

You can enjoy mountain climbing in the cool early morning air. You can descend the mountain before the sun gets too strong, so it's comfortable even in the summer.

10:00 to 13:00

This is a good time to depart after a relaxing morning. It's also a great place to enjoy lunch after climbing.

11:00 to 14:00

Although it's a slightly late start, you can enjoy the afternoon scenery while climbing. You can enjoy the beautiful scenery of the mountains illuminated by the afternoon light.

Things to bring list

・Choose climbing shoes with good grip to protect your feet.

・Please prepare clothing that can handle changes in weather, such as quick-drying and breathable shirts and pants, rainwear, and cold weather clothing.

・Bring plenty of water and high-energy snacks and meals.

・Please bring sunscreen cream and a hat to protect yourself from sunburn.

・We recommend bringing a paper map as there is no signal reception.

"Heads Up"

・By applying for the course, your mountain climbing notification will be sent to the Nara Prefectural Police.

・Please help us by sending the notification of descent after descending the mountain. There is a risk that a search party will be sent out to find you if you have descended the mountain.

・We recommend wearing long sleeves and long pants as there is a risk of being bitten by mountain leeches.It is more effective to tuck the hem of your pants into socks.

・Use insect repellent spray. Spray your shoes and clothes thoroughly with insect repellent spray to prevent leeches.

・If you are lost, don't panic, listen to the sound of the river and waterfall, and descend along the river.
